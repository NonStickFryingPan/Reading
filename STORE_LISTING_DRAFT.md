## Short Description

## Full Description

Reading is a lightweight RSS and Atom reader focused on speed, calm design, and simple source control.

Follow built-in sources or add your own feed URLs, reorder active feeds, save articles as bookmarks, and open stories in the in-app article view or your browser. Reading keeps your feed list, bookmarks, and settings locally on your device and does not include analytics or ads.

Features:

- RSS and Atom feed support
- Custom feed URLs
- Reorderable active sources
- Bookmarks
- Dark mode
- In-app article view
- Lightweight local storage

## Data Safety Draft

- Data collection: No user data collected by the app publisher
- Data sharing: No user data shared by the app publisher
- Data processed locally: feed sources, custom feed URLs, bookmarks, feed order, dark mode preference
- Network access: used to fetch RSS/Atom feeds and load article pages selected by the user
- Ads: No
- Analytics: No
- Account creation: No
- User-generated content sharing: No
